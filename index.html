Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies!

Established in September of 2019, Pat's salmon cookies has been a cult favorite in Seattle downtown. This savory cookie is filled with delicious shredded wild Alaskan Salmon and a touch of vanilla extract to bring out the full flavor profile.
